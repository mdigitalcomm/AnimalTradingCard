Rusty Spotted Cat

The rusty spotted cat is the smallest cat in the world!

  • Size: about half the size of domestic kitty;
  • Weight: between 1.7 and 3.5 pounds;
  • Habitats: forests of Sri Lanka and India;
  • Habits: nocturnal, hunting primarily on the ground and climbing when they need to escape detection.

Due to issues such as habitat loss and landscape cultivation, the rusty-spotted cat is listed as "near threatened" by the International Union for Conservation of Nature.
